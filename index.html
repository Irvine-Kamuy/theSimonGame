In each round, A random button will light up. At the 1st round, click on the displayed button then process to the next round. After the 1st round, another button will light up and this time you have to click on the button that displayed at the 1st round and then click on the button displayed at the 2nd round. At the 3rd round, you have to click on the 1st round button, the 2nd round button and the 3rd round button in the right order, and etc.
